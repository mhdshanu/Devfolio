Literary Realm

Literary Realm is a dynamic web application designed to enrich the reading experience by allowing users to write and share reviews of books they’ve read. The platform offers a user-friendly interface where readers can easily submit their reviews and track their reading journey. One of its standout features is the integration with the Open Library API, which enables users to retrieve the ISBN value of any book. This feature simplifies the process of adding books to the platform, ensuring that users can quickly and accurately catalog their reading experiences.

In addition to review submissions, Literary Realm generates visually appealing book thumbnails using the ISBN data from the Open Library API. This feature enhances the visual appeal of the reviews and provides users with a more engaging way to browse through their collection. Overall, Literary Realm combines functionality with aesthetics, offering a seamless and interactive way for book lovers to share their insights and showcase their reading adventures.

Key Features:

  • Add Reviews: Users can write and submit reviews for the books they’ve read, sharing their personal insights and opinions.
  • ISBN Lookup: The application integrates with the Open Library API to retrieve the ISBN value of any book, facilitating easy addition of books to the platform.
  • Thumbnail Generation: Automatically generates and displays book thumbnails using the ISBN data from the Open Library API, enhancing the visual presentation of reviews.
  • Edit Reviews: Users have the ability to edit their submitted reviews, allowing for updates and corrections as needed.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Features an intuitive and easy-to-navigate design, ensuring a smooth user experience for browsing and managing book reviews.
